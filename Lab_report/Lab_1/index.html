About us

Since 2021, We have been working to rescue the homeless animal.
Our main object is to help the homeless animals.

Our mission is to end the human/street dog conflict by creating a humane and sustainable environment for animals with direct benefits to the people of Pokhera, Nepal




We provide several key programs to achieve our goals: spay/neuter, rabies vaccination, rescue and adoption, street animal feeding, and community education for rabies safety and compassion.
